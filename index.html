Frequently Asked Questions

What activities does the club do?

We hold presentations on computer science topics every week, practice for contests, host a national contest, and provide an environment for Seven Lakes students interested in computer science to connect with each other.

How do I join?

Our club is open to anyone who wants to attend our meetings. No sign up is required and we don't keep a list of members. As far as we're concerned, if you come to meetings, you're part of the club.

Do I have to pay any fees?

No! Our club is completely free to everyone. If you meet the requirements and would like to join National CS Honor Society after a year, then we ask for a small fee to cover the cost of the Honor Society.

Do I need to compete to be in the club?

Competitions are not required. We encourage everyone to give contests a try because they're fun and a great way to improve your problem solving skills, but we recognize that there wide range of interests in CS beyond competitions.
